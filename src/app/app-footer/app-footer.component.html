About

The DALN is a publicly available online archive of over 7,000 personal literacy narratives that has international reach in terms of both submitters and end users. The archive consists of a variety of files and media formats (text, video, audio) that together provide a historical record of the literacy practices and values of contributors. The project, initially developed in 2005, is currently directed by faculty from The Ohio State University and Georgia State University.
